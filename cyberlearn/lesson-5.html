Lesson 5

Safe online shopping

Online shopping is convenient, but it’s important to check that websites are genuine and that you pay in a safe way.

Scenario question

You see an advert on social media for designer trainers at 90% off.

The advert takes you to a website you’ve never heard of. What is the safest way to deal with this?

Signs of a safe website

  • Padlock symbol and https:// in the address bar.
  • Realistic prices and clear returns policy.
  • Contact details such as phone number and address.
  • Well-known retailer or good independent reviews.

Safer ways to pay

  • Use credit or debit card, or a service like PayPal.
  • Avoid bank transfers and gift cards when shopping online.
  • Never share PINs, full passwords, or one-time codes.
  • If a deal looks too good to be true, it usually is.
