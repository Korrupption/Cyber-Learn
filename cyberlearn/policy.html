Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Information We Collect

CyberLearn collects minimal information to provide you with a personalized learning experience. When you create an account, we store your name and email address locally in your browser using localStorage.

We also track your lesson progress and quiz scores to help you continue where you left off. All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Maintain your login session and account access
  • Track your progress through lessons and quizzes
  • Personalize your learning dashboard
  • Display your name and completion status

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage

All user data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • Clearing your browser data will delete your account and progress
  • Using a different browser or device will require creating a new account
  • We have no access to your stored information

Cookies and Tracking

CyberLearn does not use cookies or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect analytics, behavioral data, or browsing history. Your learning activity is private and stored only on your local device.

Security

While we do not transmit your data over the internet, we recommend using strong, unique passwords for your CyberLearn account. Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your device and browser.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • Export your data by accessing your browser's developer tools
  • Modify your account information through the settings menu

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the resources page.
